Towards the collaboration to develop the potentiality of gem and jewelry human resources

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT Director, warmly welcomes Myo Myo Thant, Vice Chairman of Gems & Jewellery Entrepreneurs Association (Yangon) and its members in occasion of visiting GIT to observe its services and discuss toward the collaboration to develop the potentiality of gem and jewelry human resources in Myanmar at GIT meeting room 6th Floor on 19 January 2017
