19 - 20 August 2013

NGJA visited GIT during August 19th - 20th, 2013

Dr. Amitha Gamage, Chairman and CEO of The National Gem & Jewelry Authority (NGJA) and Dr. E. Gamini Zoysa from Sri Lanka visited GIT during August 19th - 20th, 2013 to discuss further collaboration on gem and jewelry testing and research as well as visited GIT’s gem and precious metal laboratories, museum and library.
