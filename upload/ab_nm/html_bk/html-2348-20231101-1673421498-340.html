June 10, 2016

Techniques in jewelry Design

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT’s director, presided over certificate granting ceremony for students who completed “Techniques in Jewelry Design” course instructed by Mr. Niwat Singchanachaikul, GIT Jewelry Design Specialist. This course was designed and conducted under the collaboration of GIT, Sri Lanka Embassy, and Sri Lanka Export Development Board in order to develop potentiality of Sri Lankan gem and jewelry entrepreneurs and jewelry designers. The certificate granting ceremony was recently held at GIT Training Center on 10th June, 2016.
