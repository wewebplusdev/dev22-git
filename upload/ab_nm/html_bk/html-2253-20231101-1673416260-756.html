October 29, 2017

GIT Seminar on the topic Ruby and Sapphire from mine to the market

October 29th 2017: The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public organization) GIT has Co-operation with UBM and arranges the seminar in the Singapore Jewellery & Gem Fair 2017 on the topic “Ruby and Sapphire from mine to the market” presented by Mr. Thanapong Lhuaamporn, GIT’s Gemologist at Marina Bay Sands Expo Convention Centre, In this seminar is the co-operation on knowledges with UBM that is an important factor in order to build the confident for Gems and Jewelry in Thailand.
