19 June 2013

GIT SIGNED MOU WITH THE GEMOLOGY AND LAPIDARY CENTRE OF NAMPULA, MOZAMBIQUE

Director of the Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand, Ms. Pornsawat Wathanakul and Director of The Gemology and Lapidary Centre of Nampula, Mr. Joao Manuel Antonio have signed a MoU at a ceremony held on June 19th, 2013 during GIT visit to Mozambique. The MoU has the subject of the establishment of cooperation between two institutes, aimed at promoting the development of the gem industry in both countries, scientific and technological research and training.
