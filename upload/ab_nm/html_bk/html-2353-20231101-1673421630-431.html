June 20, 2016

GIT attend the presentation on Gem and jewelry testing effectiveness development

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, Director of The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand welcome Mr. Siripol Yodmuangcharoen, Chairman of Sub-Committee of the Office of Public Sector Development Commission (OPDC) and Mrs.Jintana Chaiyawannakarn, Sub-Committee of OPDC in occasion of visiting The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) or GIT to assess the performance of GIT’s gem and jewelry testing laboratory and attend the presentation on “Gem and jewelry testing effectiveness development” as a part of Thailand Public Service Awards judgment.
