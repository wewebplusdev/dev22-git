March 23, 2017

GIT congratulated Pakistan National Day

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, Director of The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) congratulated H.E. Dr. Sohail Khan, Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to Thailand, in occasion of Pakistan National Day (Gala Reception) at Grand Ballroom, Shangri-La Hotel Bangkok on 23rd March 2017.

Further, H.E. Dr. Sohail Khan also introduced Dr. Pornsawat to H.E. General Tanasak Patimapragorn, Deputy Prime Minister as GIT is an major governmental agency specialized in gem and jewelry. GIT has maintained good relationships with Pakistan and also has planned to corporate with Pakistan to promote gem and jewelry industry for both countries.
