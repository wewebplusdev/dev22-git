July 19, 2018

Ministry of Commerce by GIT collaborates with Mozambique government to support Thai gem and jewelry industry

Mrs. Nuntawan Sakuntanaga, Director-General of Department of International Trade Promotion welcome H.E.Ernesto Max Elias Tonela Minister of Minertal Resoures and Energy and delegates in occasion of visiting The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) or GIT to discuss on the collaboration between 2 countries to mutually support gem and jewelry industry. The meeting was also joined by Mr. Boontawee Sriprasert, GIT Deputy Director Mr. Russ Jalichandra, Ambassador of Mozambique at ITF Tower, SIlom Road
