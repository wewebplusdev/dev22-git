19 December 2013

Two world - renowned gemologist visit GIT Museum

GIT Gem and Jewelry Museum has welcomed two world - renowned gemologist, Mr. Tay Thye Sun, Director of the Far East Gemological Institute and Dr. Henry A. Hanni, Gemology Expert. They visited us on Thursday, December 19, 2013. Mrs. Wilawan Atichat, GIT Senior Advisor and Mrs. Suvilai Wachiraksasawakul, Instructor of GIT gave welcoming.
