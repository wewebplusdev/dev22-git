October 18, 2016

GIT mourn the passing of His Majesty the King, Bhumibol Adulyadej

The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organizatiton) mourns the passing of His Majesty the King, Bhumibol Adulyadej, at 1st floor, ITF Tower, Silom Road, led by Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, the Director, accompanied by executives and officers, stood nine minutes in silence to reflect the greatness of His Majesty, with our loyal love and supreme respect.

R.I.P. Our beloved King and the father of the nation, you are forever in our hearts.
