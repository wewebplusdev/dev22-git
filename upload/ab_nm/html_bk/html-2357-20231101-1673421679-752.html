August 08, 2016

Towards a Global Leader in the Gem and Jewelry Industry

Mrs. Apiradi Tantraporn, Minister of Commerce (third from left) presided over the dinner talk "Towards a Global Leader in the Gem and Jewelry Industry" at Crystal Ballroom, Holiday Inn Silom, Bangkok, Thailand. The talk was given by Dr. Gaetano Cavalieri, CIBJO President (fourth from left) who received a warm welcome from Mrs. Malee Choklumlerd, Director General Department of International Trade Promotion; DITP (second from left) and Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, Director of GIT (first from left) and Mrs. Chantira Jimreivat Vivatrat Deputy Director General DITP. The talk aims to strengthen gem and jewelry industry of Thailand.
