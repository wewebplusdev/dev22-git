China International Jewellery Fair, Beijing 2018

The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) will be attending China International Jewellery Fair, Beijing 2018, during December 13-17, 2018 at China International Exhibition Centre, at booth No.8A 410/412, GIT will provide on-site Jewelry testing service and we will have displays of Ruby & Sapphire: A collector’s guide and Ruby and Sapphire Treatments and Identification: Decades of Advancement by Assoc. Prof. Dr. Visut Pisutha-Arnond.

We look forward to seeing you. Should you require more information, please contact bd@git.or.th or visit our website at www.git.or.th
