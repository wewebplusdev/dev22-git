June 13, 2016

GEM DEPOSITS AND SUSTAINABLE MANAGEMENT OF MINES IN THAILAND AND MYANMAR

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, Director of The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand has been honorably invited by the committee of 15èmes RendezVous Gemmologiques de Paris to attend 15èmes RendezVous Gemmologiques de Paris and served as a key note speaker under the topic “GEM DEPOSITS AND SUSTAINABLE MANAGEMENT OF MINES IN THAILAND AND MYANMAR” on June 13, 2016 at ASIEM (Grand Amphithéâtre) 6, rue Albert de Lapparent, Paris. This conference was very successful and attended by a large number of interested individuals.
