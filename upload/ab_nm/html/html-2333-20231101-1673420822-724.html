June 08, 2016

GIT collaborates with Sri Lanka to develop integrated gem and jewelry industry

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, the Director of GIT, was invited by Mr. Prida Tiasuwan Board Chairman and Mrs. Prapee Sorakraikitikul Vice Chairman of Pranda Group to be an honored guest speaker to promote “Strategy of Thailand's gemstones and jewellery industry” to the distinguished audiences; Mrs. Malee Choklumlerd, Director-General, Deputy Director-General Mrs. Chantira Jimreivat Vivatrat together with assistant directors from the Department of International Trade Promotion (DITP), Ministry of Commerce of Thailand and reporters on May 28, 2016 in order to encourage Thai Gems and jewellery at Pranda Jewelry PCL.
