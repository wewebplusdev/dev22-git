October 21-23, 2019

GIT organized Roadshow to promote "International Chanthaburi Gems and Jewelery Festival 2019" in Shanghai

Mrs. Wilawan Atichart, GIT Advisor, leaded GIT Team to organize roadshow to promote "International Chanthaburi Gems and Jewelery Festival 2019" in Shanghai during 21-23 October, 2019. The meeting aims to recruit buyers and visitors to visit Chanthaburi Festival and this meeting has been participated by more than 30 buyers from leading gem and jewelry companies in China.
