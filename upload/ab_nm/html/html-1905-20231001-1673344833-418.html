April 4-6, 2017

GIT will be attending MidEast Watch & Jewellery Show 2017

The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) will be attending MidEast Watch & Jewellery Show 2017, Sharjah during April 4-6, 2017 at Expo Centre Sharjah at booth No. 1021 (Hall 4), GIT will provide on-site gem testing service. Furthermore, GIT Proudly Presents the GIT standard color master sets in this fair.

GIT is organizing a Seminar at MidEast Watch & Jewellery Show 2017, Sharjah, UAE on the topic “Into The World of Ruby and Sapphire Origin, Treatment and Quality” Presented By Mr. Jirapit Jakkawanvibul, Gemmologist of The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand, on April 6, 2017 during 05.00 pm - 05.45 pm At IAEE room, Central boulevard, Expo Centre Sharjah.

We look forward to seeing you. Should you require more information, please contact schopetch@git.or.th or visit our website at www.git.or.th
