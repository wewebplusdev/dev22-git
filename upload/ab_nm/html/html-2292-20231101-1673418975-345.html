AGTA Gemfair Tucson 2019

The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) will be attending AGTA Gemfair Tucson 2019, during February 5-10, 2019 at Tucson Convention Center at booth No. 38 (Galleria Zone), GIT will provide on-site gem testing service. Furthermore, and we will have displays of Ruby & Sapphire: A collector’s guide and Ruby and Sapphire Treatments and Identification: Decades of Advancement by Assoc. Prof. Dr. Visut Pisutha-Arnond.

We look forward to seeing you. Should you require more information, please contact schopetch@git.or.th or visit our website at www.git.or.th
