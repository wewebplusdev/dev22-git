July 19, 2017

GIT staffs welcome the new GIT director

July 19, 2017: Mr. Boontawee Sriprasert, Deputy Director of The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (GIT) and staffs congratulated and welcome Mrs. Duangkamol Jiambutr, former secretary to the Minister of Commerce, in occasion of inauguration as GIT director. Mrs. Duangkamol also emphasized on GIT vision and mission to support Thailand’s gem and jewelry industry by working side by side with both government and private sectors. The reception ceremony was held at meeting room 3rd floor, The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand, ITF Tower, Silom Road.
