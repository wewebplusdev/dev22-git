GIT discuss on collaboration between Thailand and Iran to mutually develop gem and jewelry industry

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT Director, welcome M.A. Pakseresht, Commercial consultations and business assistant, Islamic Republic of Iran - Bangkok , in occasion of visiting GIT to discuss on collaboration between Thailand and Islamic Republic of Iran to mutually develop gem and jewelry industry at GIT’s meeting room recently.
