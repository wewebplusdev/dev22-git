May 04, 2017

GIT welcome H.E. Bansarn Bunnag, Thai Ambassador in Tokyo in occasion of visiting

Mr. Boontawee Sriprasert, GIT Deputy Director (Acting for Director), Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, and Ms. Wilawan Atichart, GIT Advisor, warmly welcome H.E. Bansarn Bunnag, Thai Ambassador in Tokyo in occasion of visiting GIT to discuss on the collaboration to support Thai gem and jewelry entrepreneurs to expand market to Japan.
