August 31, 2016

Jewel Fest Club (JFC) visited GIT during August 31st,2016

The 31st of August, Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT Director, warmly welcomed Mr. Pornsert Sriorathaikul, Chairman of Jewel Fest Club (JFC), and its members in occasion of visiting GIT to observe its services and special exhibition “Lapis Philosophorum” By Victor Tuzlukov”, the world’s champion gem faceter at GIT’s Gem and Jewelry Museum.
