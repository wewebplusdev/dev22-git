Towards the collaboration to develop the potentiality of gem and jewelry resources

Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT Director, warmly welcomes Mr.Bhone Myint Aung, Vice President of Myanmar Gems and Jewellery Entrepreneurs Association and its members in occasion of visiting GIT to observe its services and discuss toward the collaboration to develop the potentiality of gem and jewelry resources and raw material in Myanmar at GIT meeting room 6th Floor on 7 September 2016
