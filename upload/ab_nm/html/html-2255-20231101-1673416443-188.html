November 04, 2017

World Ruby Forum 2017

Mrs. Apiradi Tantraporn, Minister of Commerce opened ceremony of the World Ruby Forum 2017 on November 4th, 2017. The Gem and Jewelry Institute of Thailand (Public Organization) or GIT collaborated with the public and private sectors both domestically and internationally to host the World Ruby Forum 2017, to provide insights on world ruby activities in all dimensions at Shangri-La Hotel, Bangkok.
