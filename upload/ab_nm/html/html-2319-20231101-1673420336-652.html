January 26 2016

The 1st Asian Gemological Rendez Vous in Bangkok with GIT2016!!

Dr.Pornsawat Wathanakul, the Director of GIT, warmly welcomed Mr.Henry Ho, the Founder / President of Asian Institute of Gemological Sciences (AIGS) and Jewelry Trade Center, and Mr. Didier Giard, the President of Association Francaise De Gemmologie to GIT. Mr.Didier Giard invited Dr.Pornsawat to the "15th Paris Gemological Rendez Vous" on 13th June, 2016, at the National Assembly in Paris, France. And Dr.Pornsawat warmly welcomed Mr.Didier Giard to co-organize "The 1st Asian Gemological Rendez Vous " in Bangkok with “GIT2016”, which will be held on Nov 14th - Nov 15th, 2016! The Fullmoon of GEMunity is coming soon!
