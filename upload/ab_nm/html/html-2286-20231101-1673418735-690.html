July 18, 2018

GIT collaborates with NGTC to develop color stone standard to support Thai color stone traders

Mrs. Duangkamol Jiambutr, GIT Director, signed Letter of Intent (LOI) with NGTC to mutually develop color stone standard in order to support Thai color stone traders. The signing ceremony was participated by Mr. Rachane Potjanasuntorn Chairman of the Board of Director, Ms. Malee Choklumlerd Advisory to the Board of Directors, Mr. Boonkij Jitngampang Board Committee and Mr. Witit Teeramungcalanon Minister (Commercial) ,The Office of Commercial Affairs Beijing at Shanshui Hotel, Beijing on July 18th, 2018.
