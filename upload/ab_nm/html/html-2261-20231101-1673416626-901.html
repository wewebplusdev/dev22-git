February 27, 2018

GIT warmly welcome the National Assembly and Parliamentarians Friendship Group Madagascar-Thailand

Mr. Boontawee Sriprasert, GIT Deputy Director, warmly welcome Hon.Mr.Randriamandimbisoa Felix and delegates from the National Assembly and Parliamentarians Friendship Group Madagascar-Thailand in occasion of visiting GIT to discuss on the collaborations to enhance the potential of gem and jewelry industry between Thailand and Madagascar at GIT meeting room 6 floor, ITF Tower, Silom on February 27, 2018.
