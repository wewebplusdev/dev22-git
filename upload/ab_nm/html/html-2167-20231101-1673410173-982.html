December 21, 2022

 

GIT warmly welcomed Minister of Ministry of Minerals and delegation from Tanzania

Mr. Sumed Prasongpongchai, Director of GIT, Mr. Sithichai Parinyanusorn, Deputy Director (Business) together with Mr. Thanong Leelawattanasuk, Deputy Director (Technique) warmly welcomed Hon.Dr.Doto Mashaka Biteko, Minister of Ministry of Minerals, Hon.Dunstan Luka Kitandula, Member of Parliament of Tanzania, Dr. Abdulrahman Shabani Mwaga, Commissioner for Minerals of Ministry of Minerals and delegation from Tanzania on December 21st, 2022. The main purpose associated with this visit was to establish collaborations between GIT and Tanzania Gemmological Centre for mutual benefit for gem and jewelry industry for both Thailand and Tanzania.
