December 21, 2016

Hosted GIT & CGL Co-branding Gem Identification Report Press Conference

Bangkok, December 21st, 2016: Dr. Pornsawat Wathanakul, GIT Director and Mr. Hiroyuki Yoshizawa, CEO Central Gem Laboratory hosted GIT & CGL Co-branding Gem Identification Report Press Conference at Crystal Ballroom, Holiday Inn Silom to promote the interests of the gem and jewelry industry, the consumers of Japan, as well as the exporters of Thailand, and to further the technical and scientific collaboration between GIT and CGL
