September 12, 2019

GIT hosted the seminar under the topic of "The Challenge of Gem and Jewelry Industry in the Disruptive Era"

GIT hosted the seminar under the topic of "The Challenge of Gem and Jewelry Industry in the Disruptive Era" on September 12th, 2019 during the 64th Bangkok Gems & Jewelry Fair 2019. This seminar was joined by several organizations in gem and jewelry, namely Dr. Taijin Lu from National Gemstone Testing Center (NGTC) Mr. Kinjal Shah from the Responsible Jewellery Council (RJC), Mr. Jack Cunningham from Gemfields, Mr. Prida Tiasuwan Director of CIBJO - Group C President and Chairman of the Board of Directors of Pranda Jewelry PLC. and Assoc.Prof. Dr. Chakkaphan Sutthirat GIT Advisor and Mr. Thanong Leelawatanasuk Deputy Director of GIT
