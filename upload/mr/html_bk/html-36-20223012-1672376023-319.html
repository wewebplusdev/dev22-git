Membership & Privileges
Company
The privileges offered comprise the followings:
  1. 10% discount from the gem identification and testing service fee;
  2. Discount of Baht 1,000 per person from the normal fee for the trainings participated by the institute (except for overseas courses); A member company may send in 4 employees to attend the training and each of them is entitled to discount according to the quota
  3. 5% discount from any service fees within the gem and jewelry library and exemption from the library entry fee;
  4. 10% discount from the application fee for academic seminars and academic documents In any events of special promotion, the institute will grant company members priority.
Note
  1. > The institute reserves the rights to modify any conditions, wherever applicable, without notice in advance.
Individual
The privileges offered comprise the followings:
  1. 10% discount from the gem identification and testing service fee;
  2. Discount of Baht 1,000 per person from the normal fee for the trainings participated by the institute (except for courses co-organized with oversea institutes);
  3. 5% discount from any service fees within the gem and jewelry library and exemption from the library fee;
  4. 10% discount from the application fee for academic seminars and academic documents.
Note
  1. > The institute reserves the rights to modify any conditions, wherever applicable, without notice in advance. The privileges of individual members may be exercised by members or membership card holders only.
GIT International Corporate
The privileges offered comprise the followings:
  1. Receive 10% discount from regular price for precious metal testing service.
  2. Receive 10% discount when apply for GIT training course (regular price)
  3. Receive 15% discount for GIT training course when apply for 3 people (payment must be submitted in one time).
  4. GIT reserves the right to revise ACM privilege upon at least 7 days written notice.
