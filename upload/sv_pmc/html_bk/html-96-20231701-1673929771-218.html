Gem Inspection Services Procedure

1. Workpiece destruction type Divided according to methods and tools used in the analysis as follows:

> Determination of gold purity by Fire Assay technique

> Validation of precious metal purity by ICP-OES technique

> The purity of precious metals such as gold, silver and platinum

> Harmful metal alloys in jewelry, such as nickel, lead and cadmium.

2. Non-destructive type Divided according to methods and tools used in the analysis as follows:

> Determination of the purity of precious metals by X-Ray Fluorescence Technique

> The purity of precious metals such as gold, silver and platinum

> Elements of mixed ornaments Metallurgy and raw materials

> Measuring the thickness of the plating layer by X-Ray Fluorescence Technique

> Examination of the homogeneity of Buddha images

> Precious metal laser engraving service

> Determination of diffusion rate of nickel in jewelry according to BSEN1811 standard.
